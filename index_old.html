Karl Oskar Ekvall

About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in statistics at the University of Minnesota with expected graduation in February 2019. My dissertation is broadly speaking concerned with multivariate statistics for dependent data. Under that umbrella I work on asymptotic theory for mixed models, regression models for mixed-type responses (some discrete, some continuous), and computational methods for Bayesian time series models. Before coming to Minnesota on a Fulbright scholarship I was a Ph.D. candidate in economics at the University of Gothenburg, Sweden.

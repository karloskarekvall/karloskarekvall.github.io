About me:

I'm a Ph.D. student in statistics at the University of Minnesota with an interest in multivariate statistics, time series, and computation.

My research is currently focused on multivariate models for spatio-temporal data where the response vector may be of mixed-type, e.g. some components are continuous and some are discrete. For a summary of what I've done in the past, see my CV.

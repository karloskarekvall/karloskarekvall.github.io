About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at TU Wien working with Efstathia Bura on the project Sufficient Dimension Reduction Methodology in Econometric Modeling and Forecasting. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Minnesota and was there advised by Galin Jones.

Research overview

My main interest is multivariate statistics with dependent data. Currently, I work on asymptotic theory for mixed models, regression models for mixed-type responses (some discrete, some continuous), and computational methods for Bayesian time series models.
